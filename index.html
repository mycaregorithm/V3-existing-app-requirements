Medical Terminology & Acronyms

▼

Staging Systems

TNM - Tumor, Node, Metastasis staging system
T = Tumor size, N = Lymph nodes, M = Distant metastasis
FIGO - International Federation of Gynecology and Obstetrics
Used for cervical, uterine, and ovarian cancers
AJCC - American Joint Committee on Cancer
Used for melanoma staging
WHO - World Health Organization
Used for brain tumor grading
ISS/R-ISS - International Staging System / Revised ISS
Used for multiple myeloma

Cancer Types

IDC - Invasive Ductal Carcinoma
Most common type of breast cancer
ILC - Invasive Lobular Carcinoma
Second most common breast cancer type
DCIS - Ductal Carcinoma In Situ
Non-invasive breast cancer
RCC - Renal Cell Carcinoma
Most common type of kidney cancer
GIST - Gastrointestinal Stromal Tumor
Rare tumor of the digestive tract

Biomarkers & Tests

PSA - Prostate-Specific Antigen
Blood test for prostate cancer screening
CA-125 - Cancer Antigen 125
Tumor marker for ovarian cancer
CA 19-9 - Cancer Antigen 19-9
Tumor marker for pancreatic cancer
HPV - Human Papillomavirus
Virus linked to cervical and head/neck cancers
EGFR - Epidermal Growth Factor Receptor
Gene mutation in lung cancer
ALK/ROS1 - Anaplastic Lymphoma Kinase / ROS Proto-Oncogene 1
Gene mutations in lung cancer
PD-L1 - Programmed Death-Ligand 1
Biomarker for immunotherapy treatment

Special Features

Gleason Score - Prostate cancer grading system
Scores 1-5 for each of two areas, total 2-10
Breslow Depth - Melanoma thickness measurement
Measured in millimeters from skin surface
Clark Level - Melanoma depth classification
Levels I-V based on skin layer invasion
MSI - Microsatellite Instability
DNA repair defect in colon cancer
LLS - Leukemia & Lymphoma Society
Partnership branding for myeloma module
